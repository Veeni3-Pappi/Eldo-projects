Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2–3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until blended.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: Pour in the egg mixture. Tilt the pan to spread evenly.
  4. Add fillings: Once edges are set, add your fillings on one half.
  5. Fold and serve: Fold in half and slide onto a plate. Serve immediately.
