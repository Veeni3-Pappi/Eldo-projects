Scramble Eggs Recipe

  1. Crack 2 eggs into a bowl
  2. Beat the eggs with a fork
  3. Heat a skillet over medium heat
  4. Add butter to the skillet
  5. Pour the beaten eggs into the skillet
  6. Let the eggs cook for a minute or two
  7. Gently stir the eggs with a spatula
  8. Cook until the eggs are set but still moist
  9. Serve immediately
